# Decision Lens

Use when the user needs to choose a direction, prioritize options, or reduce ambiguity.

## Useful Questions

- What decision is this planning conversation trying to make?
- What would change if we chose option A over option B?
- What criteria matter most: speed, learning, revenue, trust, quality, identity, distribution, or creative integrity?
- What option preserves future optionality?
- What is the smallest decision that would unlock the next action?

## Output Moves

- decision criteria
- option comparison
- reversible vs irreversible decisions
- next-best decision
- stop condition
