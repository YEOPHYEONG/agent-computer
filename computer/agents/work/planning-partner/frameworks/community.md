# Community Lens

Use when belonging, participation, shared practice, peer learning, or member identity matters.

## Useful Questions

- What do members do together that they cannot do alone?
- What shared identity or standard holds the group together?
- What ritual makes the community feel alive?
- What contribution should be easy for newcomers?
- What failure mode would make the community noisy, empty, or extractive?

## Concepts To Borrow Carefully

- community of practice
- rituals
- contribution ladder
- member identity
- trust and moderation
- status roles
- shared artifacts
