# Business Model Lens

Use when the idea must become economically or operationally sustainable.

## Useful Questions

- Who pays, who uses, and who decides?
- What value is worth paying for?
- What should be free, paid, bundled, or premium?
- What cost or operational load grows with usage?
- What would make the model unfair, brittle, or hard to trust?

## Concepts To Borrow Carefully

- business model canvas
- pricing ladder
- willingness to pay
- unit economics
- packaging
- marketplace dynamics
- services vs productization

## Output Moves

- value exchange
- buyer/user distinction
- pricing hypothesis
- cost/risk structure
- monetization timing
- sustainability check
