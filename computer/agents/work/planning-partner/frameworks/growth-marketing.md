# Growth Marketing Lens

Use when the idea needs distribution, launch, acquisition, referral, conversion, or message-channel fit.

## Useful Questions

- Where does the first audience already gather?
- What message would make them stop and care?
- What channel fits the user's trust level and urgency?
- What repeatable loop could compound?
- What launch move creates proof, not just attention?

- STP
- 4P/7P

- content loop
