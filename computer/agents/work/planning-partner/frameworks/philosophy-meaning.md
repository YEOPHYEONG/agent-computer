# Philosophy And Meaning Lens

Use when the idea depends on worldview, language, ethics, culture, learning, identity, creativity, or what the project means.

## Useful Questions

- What human belief does this idea depend on?
- What does this project say is valuable?
- What kind of world does this project assume or invite?
- What is the deeper definition of the thing being built?
- Where could the idea become manipulative, shallow, or incoherent?

## Concepts To Borrow Carefully

- meaning-making
- language and world-building
- ethics of influence
- agency
- attention
- identity
- ritual
- cultural symbols

## Output Moves

- worldview statement
- philosophical tension
- meaning promise
- ethical boundary
- symbolic vocabulary
- coherence check
