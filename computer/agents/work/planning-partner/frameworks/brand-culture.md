# Brand And Culture Lens

Use when meaning, identity, category, audience taste, language, or cultural timing matters.

## Useful Questions

- What category does this want to escape or redefine?
- What cultural tension does the idea ride?
- What kind of person would feel this is "for people like me"?
- What words should the brand own?
- What should the project refuse to be?

## Concepts To Borrow Carefully

- category design
- positioning
- brand archetypes
- cultural tension
- symbolic value
- audience identity
- message hierarchy

## Output Moves

- category frame
- positioning territory
- brand promise
- language map
- enemy/alternative
- cultural timing note
